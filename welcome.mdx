## Products

The Spotlight ecosystem consists of a suite of integrated products, platforms and apps designed to deliver a unified experience marketplace across mobile, web and extended reality (XR). Each product plays a distinct role while operating seamlessly with the underlying Spotlight infrastructure.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card icon="mobile" href="https://expo.dev" title="Spotlight mobile app">

  </Card>
  <Card icon="globe" href="https://nextjs.org" title="Spotlight web app">
    Spotlight Website interface is powered by [Next JS React Native Framework](https://nextjs.org)
  </Card>
  <Card icon="sunglasses" href="https://source.android.com/" title="Spotlight vision - AR glasses">
