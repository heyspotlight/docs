## Overview

The Spotlight ecosystem consists of four primary entry points:

1. Spotlight Mobile App
2. Spotlight Website
3. Spotlight Partner Platform
4. Spotlight Vision (AR Glasses)

Each option is designed for a specific type of user and level of engagement.

## Getting Started with the Spotlight Mobile App

The Spotlight mobile app is the fastest and most convenient way to access Spotlight.

### Steps

1. Download the Spotlight app from the App Store or Play Store.
2. Create your Spotlight account using email, phone number, or social login.
3. Set your location and preferences.
4. Browse available experiences near you or in any destination.
5. Book experiences directly through the app.
6. Access your bookings, tickets, and recommendations in one place.

### Best for

- Everyday users
- Travelers
- Experience discovery
- Real-time booking

## Getting Started with the Spotlight Website

The Spotlight website provides a larger, more detailed interface for planning and managing experiences.

### Steps

1. Visit the official Spotlight website.
2. Create or log into your Spotlight account.
3. Search for destinations, experiences, or categories.
4. View detailed experience information.
5. Plan and book experiences.
6. Manage your bookings and account.

### Best for

- Desktop users

## Getting Started as a Spotlight Partner

Spotlight partners can list experiences and reach a global audience.

### Steps

1. Register as a partner on the Spotlight Partner Platform.
2. Complete your business profile.
3. Submit your experience listings.
4. Add descriptions, pricing, images, and availability.
5. Publish your experiences.
6. Manage bookings and track performance.

### Benefits

- Access to Spotlight's user base
- Increased visibility
- Booking management tools
- Analytics and insights

### Best for

- Experience providers
- Businesses

## Getting Started with Spotlight Vision (AR Glasses)

Spotlight Vision provides immersive augmented reality experiences.

## Steps

1. Purchase Spotlight Vision AR glasses.
2. Connect the glasses to your Spotlight account.
3. Pair with the Spotlight mobile app.
4. Explore AR-enabled locations and experiences.
5. Access immersive overlays, navigation, and interactive content.

## Benefits

- Immersive experience layer
- Real-time contextual information
- Enhanced exploration

## Best for

- Early adopters
- Explorers
- Premium users
