## Dining:

Dining listings include establishments offering food and beverage experiences where customers can reserve tables, access curated menus or unlock exclusive partner benefits.

**Figure out what category does your dining experience fall under:**

- Restaurants (General Dining): Standard full-service restaurants focused primarily on food rather than premium ambience.
- Cafés: Coffee-first or beverage-led venues serving light meals and desserts.
- Fine Dining Venues: Premium, luxury, experience-focused restaurants with high pricing and service standards.
- Casual dining outlets: Full-service restaurants with strong ambience and mid-range pricing.
- Bars, Lounges & Gastropubs: Alcohol-led venues that also serve full food menus.
- Rooftop & Experience Venues: Dining venues primarily defined by their experience, view or unique setting.
- Dessert & Beverage Venues: Dessert-first or specialty beverage venues.
- Hotel Dining Venues: Restaurants located inside hotels.
- Fast Food & Quick Dining: Fast service restaurants with counter ordering and quick turnaround.

<Warning>
  Spotlight only supports dine-in establishments. Cloud kitchens are not eligible for onboarding.
</Warning>
