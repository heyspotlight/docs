## Dining:

Dining listings include establishments offering food and beverage experiences where customers can reserve tables, access curated menus or unlock exclusive partner benefits.

**Includes:**

- Restaurants
- Cafés
- Fine dining venues
- Casual dining outlets
- Fast food chains
- Lounges with dining
- Dessert parlours
- Rooftop restaurants
- Bars & gastropubs (with food service)
- Hotel restaurants

<Warning>
  Spotlight only supports dine-in establishments. Cloud kitchens are not eligible for onboarding.
</Warning>

## Stay

Stay listings include accommodation providers offering bookable overnight stays, packages, and curated hospitality experiences.

**Includes:**

- Hotels
- Boutique hotels
- Resorts
- Homestays (Bed & Breakfast)
- Luxury retreats
- Wellness retreats

## Events

Your event listings will include time-bound or ticketed experiences.

**Includes:**

- Music events
- Nightlife events
- Comedy shows
- Sports events
- Live performances
- Food and drink festivals
- Festivals and fairs
- Social mixers
- Film screenings
- Fitness events
- Pet events
- Conferences
- Exhibitions and expos
- Open mic events
- Cultural events
- Community events
- Special curated experiences

## Activities

Your activity listings will include bookable experiences and things to do.

**Includes:**

- Adventure activities (go-karting, trekking, ziplining, etc.)
- Workshops (art, pottery, cooking, etc.)
- Fitness classes (yoga, pilates, crossfit, etc.)
- Wellness experiences (spa, meditation, therapy sessions)
- Gaming zones & arcades
- Bowling alleys
- Escape rooms
- Indoor activity centers
- Outdoor experiences
- Hobby and learning experiences

## Movies

Your movie listings will include cinema and film-related experiences.

**Includes:**

- Cinema halls
- Multiplexes
- Premium cinemas (IMAX, 4DX, recliner theatres)
- Private screening experiences
- Film festivals
- Special premiere screenings
