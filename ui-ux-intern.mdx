**Requirements:**

- Proficiency in creating Mockups, Visual Designs, and Prototyping
- Strong skills in User Experience Design (UED) methodologies and principles
- Understanding and basic competency in Front-End Development
- Familiarity with Figma and Rive for design and animation
- Ability to work effectively in a remote and collaborative environment
- Strong communication, problem-solving, and time-management skills
